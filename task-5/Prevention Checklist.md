# 🛡️Stay Safe Online (UPI Safety Tips) - Prevention Checklist
- alway verify the sender before accepting requests
- Enable app lock or fingerprint security
- ever send money to the relative first confirm with your father or someone else
- Never scan QR code to receive money
- Remember: Receiving money does NOT require PIN
- if these fraud happens to you contact the helpline number: 1930 or you can rase the complant on there national portal : https://cybercrime.gov.in
- do not download the unknown app in your laptop or mobile which can access your data
- Never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
